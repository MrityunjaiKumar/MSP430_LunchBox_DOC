Welcome to MSP430 LunchBox's documentation!
===========================================

.. image:: _static/images/Lunchbox.png
    :align: center

MSP430 LunchBox was developed at `TI CEPD, Netaji Subhash University of Technology, New Delhi <http://cedtnsit.in/>`_. More details on design and development of this board can be found at this `blog, http://dvgadre.blogspot.com/2017/01/make-yourself-msp430-lunchbox-for-1.html <http://dvgadre.blogspot.com/2017/01/make-yourself-msp430-lunchbox-for-1.html>`_.

MSP430 LunchBox is being manufactured and marketed by `SincGrid LLP <http://sincgrid.in/>`_. 
To purchase, please visit `Sincgrid Store, LunchBox <https://store.sincgrid.com/index.php?route=product/product&path=59&product_id=68>`_.

For NPTEL course repository around MSP430 Lunchbox, please visit `https://github.com/ticepd/EmbSysDesign_NPTEL_Course <https://github.com/ticepd/EmbSysDesign_NPTEL_Course>`_.
